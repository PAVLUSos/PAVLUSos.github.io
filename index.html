THERE vs IT

THERE
There – noun and we don’t want to translate – there is a cat THERE
There + noun

It
It = this = that was my birthday (it`s car)
It + adj and verb  

      Exceptions:
1) There lived granny and daddy (fairy tales)
2) Who is it? It is Ann
3) Время what time is it? – it`s my life
4) Довжина Is it far? – it`s 340 km 

CONDITIONALS

Умовні речення 1, 2 та 3 типу

1 - If I go (Simple) – I will buy (Future Simple) – реальна дія

2 - If I went (Past Simple) - I would buy (Future in the Past)                  уявлення

3- if I had gone (Past Perfect) – I would have bought (Perfect + FinP)        – використвується лише у минулому

WISH + PAST

Wish + past(про те, що я жалкую - REGRETS)

1) I wish I lived in Paris (were sleeping now) - Зараз
2) I wish I would sleep tomorrow - Майбутній
3) I wish I had slept yesterday - Минулий

        Interesting moment:
If we complain about smth or smb – I wish you would not be so stubborn

WISH TO WANT

wish + full infinitive:
I wish to see the manager.
We wish to inform you that the train has been delayed/
wish + for + noun:
I wish for a pizza.
I wish for a baby elephant.

Wish for an irritation that can change

wish + would + infinitive verb
I wish you would make the bed.

     (I believe you can, but you don`t)
I wish you made the bed.
     (You never do and I believe this won`t change.)

wish + present

Present wish: wish + past simple verb
 I wish I were taller
 I wish I knew how to speak Chinese

Wish to send "good vibrations"

wish + object I + object 2:
I wish you luck in your exam tomorrow.
We wish you a merry Christmas.
I wish you all the best for the future.
We`d like to wish you a happy birthday.

Wish for the future

NOT `wish` but `hope`
hope + will + bare infinitive
I hope you will pass your exam.
I hope I will go on holiday next year.
